About Me


Martin Oloo was born in the in the Western part of Kenya. I have basic Graphic Designing Skills and passion to finding creative ways of solving Community challenges through my mother Organization ARC-KENYA visit our page. The Organization which deal with poverty reduction in Western part of Kenya has a number of activities including Child Care, Child Protection, Agriculture, Environmental management and Fabrication Laboratory aka FABLAB with its official name as ARO FABLAB.

I happened to be a Board Member of ARC Kenya and after seing our Fablab clossind down for over one year, I took it upon myself to investigate the causes of it's failure and the possible remedies. Armed with Passion and basic skills in designing, I resigned from my employment and joined Fablab as a Volunteer. Engaging one Electric & Electronic Engineer, I managed to bring back the operations in Fablab in minimal Level

I have got privillage to work closely with Engineering Department of Kisumu National Polytechnic, Engineers Without Borders - Norway, Jens Dyvik of Fellesverktedet Fablab- Norway among others.

For the sacrifice to stabilise our Fablab, thanks be to Fab Foundation and Fab Academy Management
